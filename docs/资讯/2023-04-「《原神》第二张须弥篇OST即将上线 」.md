「《原神》第二张须弥篇OST即将上线 」

====================

![](https://upload-bbs.miyoushe.com/upload/2023/04/17/75276539/79e2fc1cc742c49b06ccc0f2f85e4b02_5494278703079155333.jpg)

[点击前往QQ音乐平台预约《啁哳流变之砂 The Unfathomable Sand Dunes》](https://y.qq.com/n3/client/pages/reserve_v2/index.html?id=2026)
